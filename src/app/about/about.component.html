WorkForAll

About

This app is an early version of something that has the potential to help people find employment in even the harshest of times!

Usage:

- Users would have a profile that they would upload a resume / CV to

- Employers would list themselves on here indicating they are recruiting

- Unlike other job search apps, this would have a focus on allowing people that don't have very much experience, living, or assets to back themselves

WorkForAll is dedicated to helping everyone find their purpose, develop their goals, and improve themselves.
